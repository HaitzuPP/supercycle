THE SHOW

Every founder answers to the data.

Supercycle is a podcast for investors tracking the frontier of AI, robotics, and health sciences. No PR filter. No prepared deck.

How it works

01

Guest arrives with a thesis

Founders and fund managers bring a specific investment thesis, not a pitch deck. A view on where capital is going and why, with data behind it.

02

We test it against data in real time

Signals, comparable transactions, and contradicting evidence are surfaced live. Guests answer to the numbers, not around them.

03

The claim goes on the ledger

Falsifiable predictions are logged publicly and tracked quarterly. The ledger is updated as signals arrive. No claim disappears.

Who we have on

Guests are fund managers, founders, or operators with a specific, defensible view on one of Supercycle's five investment themes. Prepared talking points are not the show. If you have capital behind a thesis and are willing to be held accountable to it, reach out: jack@pylon.partners
