Format

Built from live due diligence.

Supercycle is a research podcast. Every guest is a founder or fund manager Jack is either backing or actively evaluating. Conversations start from a position in the market, not a PR brief. Guests are asked to defend their thesis with numbers, not narrative.

Each episode is structured around the investment question: what has to be true for this bet to work, and what would prove it wrong. Guests who can't answer that question clearly don't come on the show.
